Hats Manufacturing Company in Bangladesh

Are you looking for Hats & Caps Manufacturing Companies for your Organization? Dhaka Hats & Caps is the Manufacturing Company you should look into.

Dhaka Hats & Caps is a premium quality cap manufacturing factory. We are designer, manufacturer and exporter of any kinds of baseball hats, promotional hats, fashion hats and Beanie. Our MOQ is 150 pcs Caps/Hats per color of fabrics. Our lead time is 15 days. We export all over the world from East to West and North to South. We work on custom designs basis and also for the promotional works.

We care about our quality of products as well as our customers all related factors like price issue, on time shipment, standard packing and suitable transportations system.

The Caps/Hats are solely made in our own Factory. We take orders from International customers worldwide. We accept MOQ 150 pcs or more Caps/Hats per color of fabrics. We make only premium quality caps/hats. Our daily production capacity is 6,000 pieces of caps/hats. We export anywhere of the world.
